# Bad_Words: The FlexWord Blocklist

> "It's a PG-13 world; I'm just living in it."

**FlexWord** allows mild profanity and common insults but excludes language whose primary purpose is to demean or dehumanize people based on identity.

### The Philosophy: Context is Critical
As a word game, FlexWord presents a single word at a time, without a surrounding sentence to help define it. 

* **We allow** words that have widely used meanings outside of their offensive context.
    * *Example:* We allow `cripple` because it is a common verb (e.g., "to cripple the economy"), even though it can be an ableist slur in other contexts.
* **We ban** words whose main function is to demean or dehumanize people.

Some words people expect to find here arenâ€™t included on purpose. Language is messy. Since FlexWord shows words in isolation, banning context-dependent terms would punish normal English usage rather than prevent harm. 

This list is intentionally narrow. It targets hate speech and severe vulgarity, not everyday profanity or slang.
